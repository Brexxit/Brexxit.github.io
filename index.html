ABOUT US

Make Brexxit Great Again

Back on July 3rd of 2017, the original Brexit faction was founded. Over the course of DreamRaids Map 10 our founder, UltraSack, learned the ropes of Minecraft factions. Having come from a PvE background, it was difficult for him to learn the ropes. However he took his YouTube audience along for the ride, inviting them to join the Brexit faction and be a part of his change. By the time Map 10 ended, UltraSack had moved on from Brexit, transitioning to the magnolia faction. Ran by iRogue and ZeroGravity, this faction took the spirit of Brexit to heart and went on to place second in DreamRaids Map 10. From here on out, things only went up for Brexit. At the beggining of Map 11, the Brexit faction had dwindled as UltraSack was accepted onto the staff team - and spent his time making the DreamRaids server a better place.
With DreamRaids closing for good, and AtlasRaids becoming the new server, UltraSack returned to make the Brexxit Faction - a successor in every way to the original. Map 1 was a rough start, however with Map 2 beggining we know now we need more members, Brexxit is now an open faction and we want you to apply to become a member today!
